From players to writers, coders to illustrators... If you've ever wanted to create a video game, there's never been a better time. I'm Kirk M. (aka @saricden), and it's my goal to create memorable Open Source Games that can be freely taken apart and repurposed, learned from, or contributed to by anybody. Let's build something great!

We apply: unrestrictive, open source & collaborative technologies to the most interactive digital medium there is.

Our games bear no copyright and instead, belong to the public domain giving you the freedom not only to play, but to extend, remix, contribute, and learn. Free forever.
